📰AI-Powered Fake News Detection System Using Machine Learning

📌Project Overview

The AI-Powered Fake News Detection System is a Machine Learning-based application designed to identify whether a news article is real or fake. The system uses Natural Language Processing (NLP), TF-IDF Vectorization, and Logistic Regression to analyze news content and provide accurate predictions. The solution helps reduce misinformation and promotes reliable information sharing.

❗Problem Statement

The rapid growth of social media and online news platforms has increased the spread of fake news and misinformation. False information can influence public opinion, create panic, and negatively impact society. Manual verification is difficult due to the large volume of content shared daily.

💡Proposed Solution

The proposed system uses Machine Learning and NLP techniques to automatically classify news articles as Real News or Fake News.
